Labeled with the patient's name, labeled "uterus and cervix", and received in formalin is a 39.6 gram hysterectomy specimen consisting of a previously posteriorly opened uterus. The uterus measures 7.0 x 3.0 x 3.0 cm. The ectocervix measures 1.8 x 0.5 cm.
