Interpretation guidelines for EGFR Gene Copy Number by FISH*

Ratio of EGFR to the centromere of chromosome 7:
Loss than 2.0: No EGFR amplification
Greater than 2.0: EGFR amplification

Hyperploidy: Greater than two centromere copies/cell.

SNR: The average number of EGFR signals per analyzed cell within the targeted region.

Clinical studies show high EGFR gene copy number by FISH analysis to be associated with favorable clinical benefit (clinical response, stable disease, time to progression, and survival) in patients with advanced non-small cell lung cancer treated with EGFR tyrosine kinase inhibitors.
